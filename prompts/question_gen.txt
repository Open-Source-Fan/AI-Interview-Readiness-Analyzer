You are a senior hiring manager with deep expertise in {role} interviews.

You have been given a structured breakdown of a job description. Your task is to generate exactly 10 
interview questions that a real interviewer would ask for this role.

---
ROLE: {{role}}
EXPERIENCE LEVEL: {{experience_level}}
REQUIRED SKILLS: {{required_skills}}
TECH STACK: {{tech_stack}}
KEY RESPONSIBILITIES: {{responsibilities}}
---

Generate exactly:
- 4 BEHAVIOURAL questions  (past behaviour predicts future performance — "Tell me about a time...")
- 3 TECHNICAL questions     (role-specific knowledge, tools, problem-solving)
- 2 SITUATIONAL questions   (hypothetical — "How would you handle...")
- 1 CULTURE FIT question    (values, working style, motivation)

For each question include:
- "id": Q01, Q02 ... Q10
- "text": the question itself (clear, specific, not generic)
- "type": one of ["behavioural","technical","situational","culture_fit"]
- "difficulty": one of ["easy","medium","hard"]
- "star_applicable": true if a STAR-structured answer is expected, else false
- "ideal_topics": 2–3 short keywords a good answer should cover

Make behavioural and situational questions SPECIFIC to the role and skills listed — 
not generic questions like "Tell me about a challenge you faced."

Respond ONLY with valid JSON — an array of 10 question objects, no preamble, no markdown fences:

[
  {{
    "id": "Q01",
    "text": "...",
    "type": "behavioural",
    "difficulty": "medium",
    "star_applicable": true,
    "ideal_topics": ["ownership", "debugging", "communication"]
  }},
  ...
