{{question}}

{{answer}}

Score each STAR component from 0 to 10 using these exact criteria:

SITUATION (0–10):
  0–2  = No situation described at all
  3–5  = Vague context mentioned but lacks specifics (no timeframe, no company/team context)
  6–8  = Clear situation with reasonable detail
  9–10 = Vivid, specific situation — timeframe, context, stakeholders all clear

TASK (0–10):
  0–2  = No personal responsibility stated
  3–5  = Responsibility implied but not explicitly stated
  6–8  = Clear ownership of a specific task or challenge
  9–10 = Explicit ownership with clear scope and why it was their responsibility

ACTION (0–10):
  0–2  = No actions described
  3–5  = Actions mentioned but vague — "I helped", "I worked on"
  6–8  = Specific actions described in first person
  9–10 = Multiple concrete steps, decision rationale explained, shows initiative

RESULT (0–10):
  0–2  = No outcome mentioned
  3–5  = Vague outcome — "it went well", "the team was happy"
  6–8  = Clear positive outcome with some specifics
  9–10 = Quantified result (%, $, time saved) or clear measurable impact stated

For each component, also extract a SHORT EVIDENCE QUOTE (max 12 words) directly from the answer
